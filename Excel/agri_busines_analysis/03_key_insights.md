# Section 3 — Key Insights

[← Back to README](../README.md)

---

## Insight 1 — Infrastructure Projects Drive Majority of Revenue

Infrastructure services (Polyhouse, Net House, Fan & Pad, Hi-Tech Smart Polyhouse) account for the **largest share of total investment**, despite representing fewer installations than maintenance contracts.

| Category | Investment Share | Avg Project Value |
|---|---|---|
| Infrastructure | ~46–50% | ₹3,00,000 – ₹25,00,000+ |
| Maintenance | ~25–30% | ₹6,000 – ₹32,000 |
| Consultancy | ~18–22% | ₹7,500 – ₹1,50,000 |
| Irrigation | ~7–10% | ~₹3,00,000 |

**Why it matters:** A single Polyhouse installation generates more revenue than ~100 annual maintenance contracts. The business model is fundamentally **high-ticket infrastructure-led.**

---

## Insight 2 — Medium Farmers Are the Core Revenue Segment

Despite Small farmers being the majority (53%), **Medium farmers (2–5 acres) generate nearly half of all investment.**

| Farmer Type | Count | Share | Investment Contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| Small | 232 | 53% | ~28–32% |
| **Medium** | **218** | **36%** | **~48–52%** |
| Large | 31 | 11% | ~18–22% |

**Why:** Medium farmers have enough land to qualify for high-value infrastructure, can meet co-investment requirements after subsidy, and represent the largest addressable market for protected cultivation technologies.

---

## Insight 3 — Protected Cultivation Dominates Infrastructure Demand

Top infrastructure services by adoption:

| Service | Unit Price | Primary Buyers |
|---|---|---|
| Polyhouse 1000 sqm | ₹7,28,000 | Medium + Large |
| Net House 1000 sqm | ₹3,00,000 | Small + Medium |
| Fan & Pad System 1000 sqm | ₹9,00,000 | Medium + Large |
| Hi-Tech Smart Polyhouse | ₹2,50,000/unit | Large only |
| Greenhouse 1000 sqm | ₹20,000 | Small farmers |

Polyhouse and Net House installations together account for **over 40% of infrastructure projects** — reflecting strong market demand for climate-controlled growing environments.

---

## Insight 4 — Government Subsidies Are a Critical Adoption Driver

Subsidy coverage reduces the farmer's effective cost significantly:

**Polyhouse example (₹9,00,000 installation):**
- Small farmer pays: ₹3,60,000 (40% of cost) | Government covers: ₹5,40,000
- Medium farmer pays: ₹4,50,000 | Government covers: ₹4,50,000
- Large farmer pays: ₹5,40,000 | Government covers: ₹3,60,000

**Dashboard shows:** Total Subsidy = ₹30,21,63,400 out of ₹64,97,78,250 total investment — subsidies cover **~46% of all infrastructure project costs.**

---

## Insight 5 — Infrastructure Projects Create Operational Bottlenecks

Average completion times vary dramatically by service type:

| Service Type | Avg Completion | Operational Pressure |
|---|---|---|
| Maintenance | 1–3 days | Low |
| Greenhouse | 7–10 days | Low |
| Net House | 15–20 days | Medium |
| Fan & Pad | 18–24 days | Medium-High |
| Polyhouse | 28–35 days | **High** |

**Dashboard shows:** Network average = **71.78 days** — pulled up by polyhouse and fan & pad installations. This indicates project scheduling challenges and labour dependency in peak seasons.

---

## Insight 6 — Southern States Lead Agri-Tech Adoption

State-level investment is heavily concentrated in Karnataka and Tamil Nadu:

| State | Approx Project Share |
|---|---|
| Karnataka | ~35–38% |
| Tamil Nadu | ~26–28% |
| Madhya Pradesh | ~18–20% |
| Maharashtra | ~15–18% |
| Telangana | ~7–9% |

Southern states have stronger horticulture ecosystems, better subsidy implementation infrastructure, and higher farmer awareness of protected cultivation technologies.

---

## Insight 7 — Consultancy is a Customer Acquisition Engine

Consultancy contributes only ~10–15% of direct revenue, but serves a strategic role — crop planning, nutrient management, and yield optimisation packages **frequently precede infrastructure investments.**

Consultancy acts as the **entry product** that builds trust and creates the pathway to high-ticket polyhouse and fertigation system sales.
